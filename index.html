More Garlic

Turn any recipe into a smart grocery list.

Everything you need to shop smarter

📥

Import from anywhere

Snap a photo of a cookbook page, paste a link or block of text, or send a recipe straight from Safari with the iOS Share Sheet. More Garlic reads it into a clean, editable card.

🛒

Smart, aisle-sorted list

Your ingredients become a grocery list grouped the way the store is laid out — produce, dairy, pantry — so you shop in one smooth lap instead of doubling back.

🍲

Combine meals, cut waste

Plan a whole week into one trip. More Garlic merges duplicate ingredients across recipes and nudges you toward sizes that leave less to spoil in the fridge.

⚖️

Scale any recipe

Cooking for two or for twelve? Adjust the servings and every quantity recalculates instantly — no mental math at the counter.

🥗

Per-serving nutrition

See approximate calories and macros per serving, estimated on-device from bundled USDA data. A quick gut-check, not a clinical figure.

🌍

On-device translation

Found a recipe in another language? Translate it right on your device, in 10 languages, without sending the text anywhere.

Private by design

More Garlic works fully offline and runs entirely on your device. There is no account to create and no analytics, tracking, or ads. Your recipes, lists, and notes stay on your iPhone — they are never collected by us.

One more thing

Tap the More Garlic button on any recipe and it doubles the garlic. You're welcome.
